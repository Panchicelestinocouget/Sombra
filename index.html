9 ingredients.

Shea Butter

Moisturizing and nourishing. Keeps your skin from drying out in the sun.

Cocoa Butter

High melting point. Holds up in tropical heat. Smells like chocolate.

Beeswax

Creates a water-resistant barrier so it stays on in the water.

Coconut Oil

Lightweight. Absorbs easily into the skin.

Non-Nano Zinc Oxide

The only active ingredient. Reflects UVA + UVB. Safe for reefs.

Rhassoul Clay

Moroccan red clay. Absorbs excess oil. Gives the warm tint.

Cacao Powder

Natural pigment for a warm, bronzed look. Antioxidant.

Cinnamon

Adds a golden undertone and a subtle warm scent.

Carrot Seed Oil

Rich in beta-carotene. Antioxidant that helps protect the skin.
